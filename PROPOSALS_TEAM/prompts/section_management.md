# Management Approach Section Prompt

## Objective
Demonstrate how you'll manage the program, communicate, ensure quality, and staff appropriately.

## Key Components

### 1. Governance Structure
- Organizational chart (RACI-aligned)
- Decision-making authority
- Escalation paths
- Client touchpoints

### 2. Reporting & Communication
- Frequency (daily/weekly/monthly)
- Formats (dashboards, status reports, QBRs)
- Stakeholder matrix
- Tools (Jira, Confluence, MS Project)

### 3. Quality Assurance
- QA methodology (Agile, Six Sigma, CMMI)
- Inspection points
- Acceptance criteria
- Continuous improvement process

### 4. RACI Matrix
| Activity | Responsible | Accountable | Consulted | Informed |
|----------|-------------|-------------|-----------|----------|
| ... | ... | ... | ... | ... |

### 5. Staffing Model
- Team structure
- FTE allocation
- Key personnel roles
- Subcontractor management (if applicable)

## Federal-Specific
- Reference FAR clauses on subcontracting (19.7, 52.219-14)
- CPARS compliance approach
- Government oversight accommodation
- Security clearances (if required)

## Enterprise-Specific
- Agile/DevOps integration
- SLA commitments
- Change management framework
- Client success metrics

## Proof Requirements
- Similar program management experience
- Tools/certifications (PMP, Agile, ITIL)
- Team retention metrics
- Client satisfaction scores

## Style
- Answer-first for each requirement
- Tables for RACI, staffing, reporting schedule
- One-page org chart with narrative
- So-What: "This structure ensures rapid response and continuous alignment with [Client] priorities."
