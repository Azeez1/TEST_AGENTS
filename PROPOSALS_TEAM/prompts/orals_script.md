# Orals Presentation Script Prompt

## Objective
Create a 12–15 slide deck with speaker notes and a Q&A bank for oral presentations (common in federal Best Value proposals).

## Deck Structure

### Slide 1: Title
- Company name
- RFP/Solicitation number
- Date
- Team leads present

### Slides 2-3: Executive Summary
- Understanding of mission/problem
- 3 Win Themes (visual icons)
- Key outcomes with numbers
- Why us in one sentence

### Slides 4-8: Technical Approach
- Answer the top 3–5 eval factors
- One major point per slide
- Graphics > text (process flow, architecture)
- Proof callouts on each slide

### Slide 9: Management & Staffing
- Org chart
- Key personnel photos/bios (1-line each)
- RACI summary
- Communication plan

### Slide 10: Past Performance
- 2–3 relevant examples
- Results in big numbers
- Client logos (if allowed)
- Reference availability

### Slide 11: Transition (30/60/90)
- Timeline visual
- Day 1 readiness
- Risk mitigation highlights

### Slide 12: Security/Compliance
- Certifications (SOC2, ISO27001, CMMC, FedRAMP)
- Federal: NIST 800-171, 508 compliance
- Enterprise: CAIQ/SIG status

### Slide 13: Pricing/Value
- Model summary (no detailed pricing unless required)
- Cost drivers
- Value proposition
- Options/flexibility

### Slide 14: Why [Your Company]
- Recap 3 Win Themes
- Differentiators
- Client benefit

### Slide 15: Q&A
- "Questions?"
- Contact info

## Speaker Notes (Per Slide)
- 1–2 minutes max per slide (total = 20–30 min)
- Answer-first format
- Anticipate questions
- Practice transitions

## Q&A Bank (10 Questions)

Prepare model answers for:

### Federal Orals
1. How will you handle [specific risk]?
2. Describe your past performance on similar programs.
3. What if key personnel leave?
4. How do you ensure 508 compliance?
5. Explain your subcontracting approach (if FAR 19.7 applies).
6. How will you transition in 30 days?
7. What's your QA process?
8. How do you handle security incidents?
9. What tools/methodologies will you use?
10. Why should we choose you over competitors?

### Enterprise Orals
1. How will you demonstrate ROI in the pilot?
2. What's your integration approach?
3. How do you handle data privacy (GDPR/CCPA)?
4. Describe your change management process.
5. What if the pilot doesn't meet KPIs?
6. How do you ensure security at scale?
7. What's your support model?
8. How do you handle vendor lock-in concerns?
9. What differentiates you from [known competitor]?
10. Can you provide client references?

## Format Notes
- **Slides:** High contrast, 24pt+ font, minimal text
- **Graphics:** One idea per visual, So-What caption
- **Branding:** Consistent colors, professional
- **Accessibility:** Alt text on all images, readable fonts

## Delivery Tips
- Rehearse 3+ times
- Assign speakers to slides
- Time each section
- Prepare backup slides (not in main deck)
- Have printed handouts ready (if allowed)
