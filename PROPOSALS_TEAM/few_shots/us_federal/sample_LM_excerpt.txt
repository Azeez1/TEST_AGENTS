SECTION L – INSTRUCTIONS TO OFFERORS:
Offerors shall submit one (1) PDF not exceeding 25 pages, 11-pt font, 1-inch margins.
All proposals must include a table of contents and be submitted via SAM.gov no later than
January 30, 2025, 2:00 PM EST.

Offerors shall provide a 30/60/90 transition plan and identify key personnel with resumes.
All certifications and representations must be current as of the proposal due date.

SECTION M – EVALUATION:
Factor 1: Technical Approach (Most Important)
Factor 2: Management Approach (Equally Important to Factor 3)
Factor 3: Past Performance (Equally Important to Factor 2)
Factor 4: Price (Least Important)

The Government shall make a best-value tradeoff determination. Past Performance shall
be evaluated per FAR 15.305. Proposals with no relevant past performance will be
evaluated neither favorably nor unfavorably per FAR 15.305(a)(2)(iv).

Technical Approach will be evaluated for understanding of requirements, feasibility
of proposed solution, innovation, and risk mitigation strategies.

Offerors must demonstrate 24/7 operations capability and achieve 99.9% uptime SLA.
