## Step 3: Research Phase (Agent-Driven, 30-60 minutes)

The agent will automatically:

### Phase 1: Qualification
- Assess win probability
- Check resource availability
- Determine strategic fit
- Make Go/No-Go recommendation

### Phase 2: Deep Research
**Client/Agency:**
- Background and mission
- Recent initiatives and priorities
- Key decision-makers
- Budget and funding sources

**Competitive Intelligence:**
- Known competitors
- Their strengths/weaknesses
- Market positioning
- Differentiation opportunities

**Requirements Analysis:**
- Build compliance matrix (government)
- Map evaluation criteria (enterprise)
- Identify pain points (private)
- Note all deadlines and constraints

**You can help by providing:**
- Links to RFP documents
- Your company's past performance
- Available team members
- Your unique capabilities

## Step 4: Strategy & Creation (Agent-Driven, 2-4 hours)

The agent will:

- Create 3-5 win themes
- Develop value proposition
- Plan differentiation approach
- Outline key messages

### Create Content
**Executive Summary** (agent writes last, you review first)
- Problem understanding
- Your solution
- Unique value
- Key differentiators

**Technical/Solution Sections**
- Methodology and approach
- Deliverables and timeline
- Resources and team

**Qualifications**
- Relevant past performance
- Case studies with metrics
- Team credentials
- Company capabilities

**Pricing/Commercial**
- Cost breakdown
- Value justification
- ROI demonstration

### Your Role
Review drafts and provide:
- Specific examples from past projects
- Exact metrics and results
- Client testimonials (if available)
- Technical details or constraints
- Pricing guidance

## üö® Common First-Time Mistakes

### Mistake 1: Starting Too Late
**Problem:** Rushing leads to errors and generic content
**Solution:** Start 2+ weeks before deadline minimum

### Mistake 2: Not Reading RFP Thoroughly
**Problem:** Missing critical requirements = rejection
**Solution:** Read completely before starting, take notes

### Mistake 3: Using Generic Content
**Problem:** Templated proposals rarely win
**Solution:** Research deeply, customize everything

### Mistake 4: Weak Executive Summary
**Problem:** Decision-makers may only read this section
**Solution:** Write last, review first, make it compelling

### Mistake 5: No Evidence for Claims
**Problem:** Unsubstantiated claims aren't trusted
**Solution:** Every statement needs proof (metrics, testimonials, case studies)

### Mistake 6: Poor Proofreading
**Problem:** Typos kill credibility instantly
**Solution:** Multiple review rounds, fresh eyes, spell check

### Mistake 7: Missing Deadline
**Problem:** Late = automatic rejection
**Solution:** Submit 24+ hours early, verify receipt
